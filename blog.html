Technology is so amazing. Last night I was having to study for a plant ID quiz and the pictures of them were on my phone. My phone is far from a smart phone, it is a slider phone. Anyways so it has a smart screen and not the best quality pictures. I really wanted to get them on my computer which has a bigger screen, My friend was like, "send them over via bluetooth." Entry #3

This weekend I went camping with Hort Club. Travel is such an awesome thing. We traveled by cars, which this camping trip would not have happened if cars were never invented. Anyways it was a long drive but totally worth it. It was like we went back in time because of all the old technology in the botanical garden. They had a water wheel running in a little river . It was cool to see how it was run. Also in the house we stayed in, there was a room of all this old antiques. There was a loom, a camera, and an apple peeler. It was cool to see the stuff and what the room would look like from that era.
