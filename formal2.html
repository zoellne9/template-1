Introduction

The discipline of Horticulture is an interesting area of study. Horticulture is the art and science of growing and cultivating plants. The word comes from the Latin word Horus, meaning enclosed garden. An example of this is how the company called OFA merged with ANLA which is the American Nursery and Landscape Association, “One Industry, One Voice” (McKellar). The two companies have created amazing programs for Horticulture like the OFA scholars, a program to honor new students doing well in the world of Horticulture (McKellar). The industry is always changing. There are always new plants coming out and people coming up with new ways to grow them, so communication through trade shows is key. Also the research done to make the plants is very tedious work. Finally technology is used to grow the plants, kind of like how some plants could not be grown here in Michigan without greenhouses.
