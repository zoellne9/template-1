Scientific Paper

The Effects of Nitrogen Fertilization on the Expression of Slow-Mildewing Resistance in Knox Wheat is a scientific paper I have done work with. In summary, the paper is about how does fertilizing effect the growth of the disease powdery mildew on wheat (Shaner and Finney 1977). This paper is a great example of both research and communication. For research it is an experiment and explains how to do an experiment. It is even better for communication because it show the layout of a scientific paper and also how having done this research has showed that adding nitrogen to wheat can increase growth of the plant but also increases the probability of powdery mildew. So it just told the industry to find a way to get a way around it, as in do more research, or just to let farmers outweigh the risk of disease with more growth.
