Analysis of Artifact

Phones were meant to bring us together by allowing a person to contact another person from far away in an instant. But think how one person actually communicates with someone else… we sometimes call, most of the older generations do, but most of the time we text, which is most of what my generation does. Actually texting is the most common form of communication according to Thompson (2013). Phones are good because they empower the user and can open a portal to the online world (Information and Communications for Development 2012). But people just misuse it. Finally a phone can lead to major opportunities in advances for human development, if people would use it for that instead of playing games (Information and Communications for Development 2012).
