about me

My name is Preston Zoellner. I am a Freshman a Michigan State University. My major is Horticulture, which is the science and art of growing fruits, vegetables, flowers, or ornamental plants. I plan on specializing in Viticulture which is the science, production, and study of grapes. My end goal is to own a winery on the Old Mission Peninsula (which is where I live now). I have a job at Brys Estates Vineyard and Winery as a cashier and field worker. I am in Horticulture Club here at MSU.
