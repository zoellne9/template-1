Conclusion

In conclusion, Horticulture is a great fit for me. This is because it mixes both art and science. It has many cool things about it, like how we communicate through trade shows and you need to brand yourself. Also that technology is a big part of the plant world, even though we are all about green! Finally I loved doing the research for my major because you just get to mess around with plants and see what cool combinations you get. Horticulture is a wonderful discipline.
